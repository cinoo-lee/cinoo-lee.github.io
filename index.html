About

I'm a Senior Applied Scientist at Microsoft, working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and social psychology.

I completed my Ph.D. in Social Psychology at Stanford University, advised by Jennifer Eberhardt, Hazel Markus, and Dan Jurafsky. Before that, I earned my B.A. in Psychology with honors from Rice University.

My work has been featured in MIT Technology Review, The Guardian, NPR, and Venture Beat.
